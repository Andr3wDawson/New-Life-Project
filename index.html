SERVICE TIMES

New Life Millbrook exists to build a loving community within our River Region and to equip believers to expand the kingdom of God.

We know that God values relationship. Throughout the Bible, we see that God had relationship with men and women. He has not changed and still desires to have relationship with His children. It is also important to experience the joy of relationship with others. At New Life, we value deep, lasting connections to be developed because we need each other to fulfill our destinies!
